Setting Up a VPN on Your iPhone

There are three primary ways to set up a VPN on your iPhone: manually, through an app, and through a browser.

Manual Configuration

If you've been given a VPN setup, navigate to the Settings app, find the VPN on the left side, and click on the add VPN configuration button.

You'll need to enter the server, remote ID, username, and password provided by your employer or VPN service.

Once you've entered the information, click done, and the VPN configuration will be saved in the VPN tab.

Setting Up a VPN through an App

You can also set up a VPN using a VPN app, such as NordVPN.

Download the app from the App Store, sign up on your computer, and log in to the app on your phone.

The app will ask for permission to set up a VPN configuration, which will automatically configure the VPN for you.

To connect to the VPN, choose a server from the list and click on it.

Setting Up a VPN through a Browser

You can also set up a VPN through a browser, such as the Tor browser.

Download and install the Tor browser, and open it on your phone.

The Tor browser will allow you to connect to a VPN server in one of many countries, anonymizing and encrypting your connection.
