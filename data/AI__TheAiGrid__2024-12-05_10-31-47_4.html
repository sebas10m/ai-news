Amazon Nova: New Frontier Models

Amazon has introduced its own frontier models that deliver frontier intelligence and industry-leading price performance.

Models Overview

There are four flavors of the intelligence set of models:

  • Micro: a text-only model that feeds text and outputs text, laser-fast, and cost-effective.
  • Light: a multimodal model that inputs text or image and outputs text.
  • Pro: a multimodal model that inputs text or image and outputs text, with a focus on industry-leading intelligence.
  • Premer: a largest multimodal model that inputs text, image, or video and outputs text, image, or video.

Benchmarks

The models have been benchmarked against leading models in the industry, including Lama and Google's Gemini.

  • Micro model: performs better than Lama and Gemini in raw numbers.
  • Light model: performs similarly to OpenAI's GPT 4.0 Mini.
  • Pro model: performs better than GPT 4.0 in 17 out of 20 benchmarks.

Features and Integration

The Nova models are integrated with Bedrock's knowledge bases, allowing for fine-tuning, distillation, and RAG capabilities.

The models are also optimized to work with proprietary systems and APIs, enabling multiple orchestrated automatic steps and agentic behavior.

New Features

Amazon Nova Canvas is a state-of-the-art image generation model that inputs natural language text and outputs images.

Amazon Nova Reel is a state-of-the-art video generation model that inputs natural language text and outputs videos.

Future Developments

Amazon plans to release a speech-to-speech model in Q1 and an any-to-any model mid-year, enabling multimodal-to-multimodal interactions.

To provide selection and choice, AWS will offer a range of models from partnerships, model providers, and its own models, allowing users to select the best combination for their needs.
