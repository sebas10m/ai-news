Data-Intensive Teams

Elias, from Olex Group, talks about data-intensive teams.

Introduction

Elias introduces himself and Olex Group, a large online marketplace.

Elias explains that data-intensive teams are those for whom running data-intensive applications and data products is the main challenge in their work.

Challenges of Data Products

Elias discusses the challenges of developing data-intensive products, including the need for larger development investments upfront and the uncertainty of return on investment.

Rapid Prototyping and Experimentation

Elias explains the importance of rapid prototyping and experimentation in data-intensive teams.

Elias introduces the concept of a "chameleon," representing the uncertainty of return on investment, and the importance of minimizing investment in prototyping.

Elias discusses the typical development flow for data products, which can lead to costly failures.

Making the Universe a Better Place

Elias discusses the importance of building a robust infrastructure and operations layer in data-intensive teams, using the metaphor of a "elephant," representing the need for a solid foundation.

Elias explains that building a system that can survive at least an order of magnitude of growth is crucial for data-intensive teams.

Conclusion

Elias concludes by emphasizing the importance of building a robust infrastructure and operations layer in data-intensive teams, and the need for rapid prototyping and experimentation.
