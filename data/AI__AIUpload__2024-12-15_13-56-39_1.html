Introducing Android XR

Android XR is a platform to extend your reality, explore, connect, and create in new ways.

Main Features

  • New platform for headsets and glasses
  • Best tools for developers to use, reducing friction
  • Collaboration with Samsung and Qualcomm

Key Areas

  • Extended Reality (XR) for a natural user experience
  • Conversational AI with Gemini for a multimodal experience
  • Natural input like gaze and hand for seamless interaction

Gemini Capabilities

  • Reasoning based on real-world inputs
  • Spacializing 2D content for a immersive experience
  • Opening 2D apps like Maps with deep intents
  • Automatic photo and video spatialization for memories

Benefits

  • Imagine being able to navigate cities and landmarks with ease
  • Get real-time translation while traveling
  • Use conversational AI for everyday tasks

Conclusion

Android XR is a game-changer for the future of augmented reality. With Gemini, you'll experience a new level of natural interaction and conversational AI.
