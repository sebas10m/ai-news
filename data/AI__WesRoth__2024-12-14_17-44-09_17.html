Google Joins Forces with Samsung and Qualcomm to Unleash Android XR

Android XR is an open platform that combines years of investment in artificial intelligence and virtual reality to bring next-gen experiences to headsets and glasses.

The Perfect Time for XR

The underlying technology allows us to process inputs from all different kinds of signals, much like you process the real world.

Android XR is our new platform for headsets and glasses, offering some of the best tools for developers to use, and it reduces a lot of the friction.

Headsets and Glasses

We think about headsets as episodic products that you put on for a specific reason, like watching a game, and glasses as all-day products that you use to get value throughout your day.

With glasses, you'll feel like you're a local, no matter where you are, and our technology really enables us to shrink down the displays that go into the glasses.

Collaboration and Ecosystem

Building a powerful XR platform requires deep collaboration between software, UX, and hardware partners, as well as an ecosystem of developers, designers, and device makers.

The Future of XR

Android XR will enable seamless interactions with AI voice assistants, giving them access to the world, and the world of the AI voice assistants.

Users will be able to perform tasks like watching tutorials with feedback, asking questions about cooking, or even taking phone calls and answering text messages.

The Android ecosystem is massive, with billions of users and an open source operating system, making Android XR an open unified system for XR headsets and glasses.
