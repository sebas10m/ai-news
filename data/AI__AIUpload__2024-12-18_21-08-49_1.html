Google Announcements

Astra AI Voice Assistant

Astra is the AI voice assistant that has been upgraded and improved, including live coaching as you're playing video games. It can walk you through how to cook something, how to chop something up, and it knows the recipes.

Project Mariner

Project Mariner is a research prototype that automates tasks in your browser, acting like an AI agent by carrying out tasks. It can find flights and hotels, shop for household items, find recipes, and other tasks that currently require users to click through the web.

Project Gemini 2.0

Project Gemini 2.0 is a model that can natively generate images as part of your conversation. It can also generate text, and it can understand and respond to multimodal input.
