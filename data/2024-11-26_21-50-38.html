Text-to-Image Models

Black Forest Lab released Flux.1 Tools, a suite of models for text-to-image generation, enabling image editing and expansion.

  • Flux.1 Fill: state-of-the-art in-painting and out-painting models
  • Flux.1 Depth: models trained to enable structural guidance based on a depth map
  • Flux.1 Canny: models trained to enable structural guidance based on canny edges
  • Flux.1 Redux: an adapter for mixing and recreating input images and text prompts
