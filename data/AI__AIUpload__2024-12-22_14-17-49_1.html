O3 and O3 Mini: AGI Models

Today, we're excited to announce the release of our next frontier model, O3, and its smaller but equally impressive counterpart, O3 Mini. O3 is a powerful model that offers significant improvements over our previous O1 model, while O3 Mini is a cost-efficient version that achieves comparable performance at a lower cost.

O3's Capabilities

O3 is a strong model that has surpassed human-level performance in various tasks. On software-style benchmarks, O3 achieved a 71.7% success rate, outperforming its predecessor O1 by over 20%. This is a significant milestone in the development of AGI.

O3 Mini's Capabilities

O3 Mini, on the other hand, is a cost-efficient model that has been trained to achieve comparable performance to O3. In coding benchmarks, O3 Mini achieved a 58.2% success rate, beating O1's performance at a lower cost. O3 Mini also supports three different options for reasoning effort: low, medium, and high.

ARC AGI Challenges

The ARC AGI challenge is a benchmark that tests the ability of AI models to solve complex problems. O3 has achieved a new state-of-the-art score of 75.7 on the ARC AGI semi-private holdout set, surpassing human-level performance. When asked to think longer, O3 was able to score 85.7% on the same holdout set, a major milestone in the development of AGI.

O3 and O3 Mini Availability

O3 and O3 Mini are available for public safety testing starting today. We encourage developers and researchers to apply for access to these models and help us test their capabilities.

Conclusion

We're excited to share the capabilities of O3 and O3 Mini with the world. These models are a significant step forward in the development of AGI, and we're eager to see the impact they'll have on various industries and applications.

Future Plans

We're planning to post-train O3 Mini with safety interventions and are doing internal safety testing to ensure the safe and responsible deployment of these models.
