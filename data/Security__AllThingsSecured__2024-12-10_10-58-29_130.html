Migrating Away from LastPass: A Step-by-Step Guide

Please follow these steps to safely remove your data from LastPass and migrate to a new password manager.

Step 1: Export Your Data from LastPass

1. Go to LastPass and navigate to Advanced Password > Export. 2. Confirm the export by checking your email and following the link provided. 3. Once exported, download the CSV file and save it securely.

Step 2: Import Your Data into a New Password Manager

1. Open your new password manager and navigate to File > Import. 2. Select Web Importer and choose the password manager you are importing from (e.g., LastPass). 3. Upload the CSV file and follow the on-screen instructions to import your data.

Step 3: Cancel Any Subscriptions

1. Log in to your LastPass account and navigate to Account Settings. 2. Click on Cancel Subscription or Cancel Any Future Payments to cancel your account.

Step 4: Destroy All Sessions and Trusted Devices

1. Go to Account Settings and scroll down to find Advanced Settings. 2. Click on Destroy Sessions and choose to kill all but the current session. 3. Remove any trusted devices from your LastPass account.

Step 5: Transfer 2FA Sessions (Optional)

If you have 2FA enabled in LastPass, transfer your 2FA sessions to your new password manager to avoid losing access to your accounts.

Step 6: Delete Items from LastPass (Optional)

You can delete individual items from LastPass by clicking on each item and selecting Delete. However, this can be a time-consuming process.

Step 7: Remove Browser Extensions and Mobile Apps

1. Remove the LastPass extension from your browser. 2. Delete the LastPass app from your mobile device or any other mobile device you have.

Step 8: Delete Your LastPass Account

1. Go to LastPass.com/delete_account.php and follow the prompts to delete your account. 2. If you're not logged in, you can still delete your account by clicking on "Forget to delete my account" and entering your email address.
