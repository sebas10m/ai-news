Sora: AI Video Generator Model

OpenAI has finally released Sora, an AI video generator model, after years of anticipation. The model has taken a little longer to come out, but it has an edge on other AI video generators.

Features

  • Up to 50 videos per month with a ChatGPT paid account
  • Up to 500 videos per month or 10,000 credits with the $200/month pro plan
  • Generation at 720p and 1080p resolution
  • Unlimited relaxed videos with the pro plan
  • Five concurrent generations with the pro plan
  • Download videos without watermarks with the pro plan

Dashboard

The Sora dashboard is intuitive and familiar, with options to organize videos by folders, filter by prompts, and select aspect ratios.

Payload and Prompts

Sora allows for multiple prompts along a timeline, which can be used to create complex animations. The model struggles with morphing animations, but presets can help improve results.

Limitations and Future Development

The model is currently slow, taking 15-20 minutes to generate videos, and is bogged down by heavy traffic. However, Sora is still impressive, and users are already generating amazing videos with detailed prompts.

Conclusion

Sora is an exciting development in AI video generation, and with a little practice and patience, users can create impressive videos. I look forward to exploring its capabilities further and sharing my findings in future videos.
