VO2's Capabilities

VO2 is a highly advanced video generation model that can produce high-quality videos with a 4K resolution. It has extensive camera controls, including the ability to capture subtle movements and reflections. The model also demonstrates a strong understanding of physics, allowing it to generate realistic and accurate videos.
