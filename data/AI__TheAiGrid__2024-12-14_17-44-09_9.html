Google Announces Android XR: A Platform to Extend Reality

Google has announced a new platform called Android XR, which is designed to extend reality and allow users to explore, connect, and create in new ways.

Key Features and Advantages

  • Android XR is a platform that can process inputs from various signals, similar to how humans process the real world.
  • The platform offers tools for developers to create experiences that can be viewed on headsets or glasses.
  • It reduces friction and allows for more natural computing, as users can interact with virtual objects and environments in a more intuitive way.
  • With Android XR, users can access information and perform tasks in real-time, without the need for a smartphone or computer.
  • The platform can be used for a variety of applications, including navigation, translation, and assistance with tasks and activities.

Collaboration and Future Plans

Google has partnered with Samsung and Qualcomm to develop Android XR, and the companies have worked closely together to bring the platform to life.

The next device to be released will be a headset built by Samsung, which will be available for purchase next year.

This will be the first device to use the new Android XR platform, and it will be an exciting development for users who want to experience the future of augmented reality.

Conclusion

Android XR has the potential to revolutionize the way we interact with reality and access information.

With its advanced features and collaborative development, this platform is poised to change the game in terms of augmented reality and artificial intelligence.

As Google continues to develop and refine Android XR, we can expect to see even more exciting applications and advancements in the future.
