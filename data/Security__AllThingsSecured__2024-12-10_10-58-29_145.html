How Easy is it to Dig Up Your Personal Information Online?

A friend asked me for a favor, to dig up information about a guy she'd met online, who'd been coy about his identity. She gave me a blurry profile picture, a first name, and some random information from his dating profile. Within 20 minutes, I knew everything about him, including his home address, phone number, and personal details.

The Common Mistakes People Make

  • Not making social media accounts private
  • Sharing too much personal information
  • Not being aware of the publicly available information circulating online

How I Found the Information

I started with the profile picture, but it was a dead end. I then searched for the user's name, which I found in the webpage's source code. This led me to their Instagram and Facebook accounts, where I found more information.

The Power of Publicly Available Information

With a simple search, I was able to find the user's age, phone number, and home address. I then used a public records search to confirm the address and found a connection between the user and their mother.

Lessons Learned

  • Make your social media accounts private
  • Understand that your old information is circulating online and take steps to remove it
  • Recognize that online anonymity is a myth and be prepared to take steps to protect your privacy

What You Can Do

Make your social media accounts private, remove any publicly available information that's circulating online, and be aware of the risks of online anonymity. Watch this video for step-by-step instructions on how to erase your private information from the internet.
