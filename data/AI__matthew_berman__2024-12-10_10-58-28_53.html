Sora: The Revolutionary New Tool for AI-Generated Videos

Sora is the latest innovation from OpenAI, and it's a game-changer. This incredible tool allows users to create stunning AI-generated videos, but it's not just a one-trick pony. Sora is a full-featured editing suite that enables users to create, edit, and customize their videos with ease.

Features

  • Remix: Replace, remove, or reimagine elements in your video with remix.
  • Recut: Find and isolate the best frames, extending them in either direction to complete a scene.
  • Storyboard: Create a video with multiple actions across the sequence using a familiar timeline.
  • Loop: Create a video that loops back around, repeating the content seamlessly.
  • Blend: Blend two different videos together into one.
  • Story Presets: Save and reuse prompts for consistent video styles.

Tech Details

For users of ChatGPT Pro, Sora offers unlimited generations, highest resolution, 500 priority videos, unlimited relaxed videos, 1080p resolution, and 20-second duration with 5 concurrent generations.

Availability

Sora is currently available in select countries and plans are not yet available for free users, but the price of $200 a month for ChatGPT Pro users is starting to look very attractive.
