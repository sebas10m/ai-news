SaferVPN Setup Tutorial

Introduction

In this tutorial, we will be setting up SaferVPN on both a computer and a mobile device. This will include purchasing, installing, and using SaferVPN to demonstrate its features and user experience.

Computer Setup

First, we will go to the SaferVPN website and sign up for a free trial. We will receive a confirmation email and complete the signup process.

Next, we will click on the "Install the software and download now" button, which will take a few minutes to complete. Once downloaded, we will install the SaferVPN software on our computer and launch it.

The software provides a simple connect button and a list of server options. We will choose the USA East server and click connect, which will take a few seconds to complete.

The settings on the right side of the software include option for VPN protocols and a general settings section. We will also view our account information and other settings.

Next, we will use our Apple iOS device to download the SaferVPN app from the App Store. We will sign up through the app using the username and password we created earlier.

Once logged in, we will choose a server from the list and click connect, which will take a few seconds to complete. We will also view our account information and other settings.

However, in the free version of the app, we are limited to basic settings and cannot access advanced options.

Conclusion

We hope this tutorial has been helpful in demonstrating how to set up SaferVPN on a computer and a mobile device. If you have any further questions, please check out our FAQ page or other setup tutorials on our website.
