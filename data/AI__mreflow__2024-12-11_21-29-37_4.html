FlowState: A Revolutionary AI Image Generation Tool

FlowState is a groundbreaking tool from Leonardo AI that revolutionizes the way we generate images using AI. It eliminates the need for prompt engineering, allowing users to create high-quality images with ease.

Using FlowState

To use FlowState, users simply need to navigate to the Leonardo AI website, log in, and select the FlowState option. From there, they can choose from pre-set prompts or create their own, and the AI will generate a grid of images based on their input.

The key feature of FlowState is its ability to generate a wide variety of images based on a single prompt, allowing users to easily refine their search and find the perfect image. Users can scroll through the grid, clicking on images they like, and the AI will update the style and prompts to match.

Key Features

  • Revolutionary AI image generation with minimal prompt engineering required
  • Generate high-quality images with ease, using a wide range of styles and prompts
  • Refine your search with the ability to drill down on specific styles and prompts
  • Saved images can be stored in the user's library for future use
  • Generate videos and further images based on saved images
