PureVPN Setup Tutorial

This tutorial will guide you through the process of setting up PureVPN on both your computer and mobile device. We'll cover the installation process, logging in, and basic usage.

Computer Setup

To start, visit the PureVPN homepage at purevpn.com. Click on the "Get PureVPN" button to proceed.

On the next page, select your preferred subscription plan, which includes options for one month, six months, and one year. You can choose from one of these plans, which includes five devices per plan.

Next, enter your payment information. PureVPN offers a variety of payment methods, including PayPal, BitPay, Alipay, and gift cards.

After completing the payment process, you'll be directed to your PureVPN dashboard. From here, you can download the PureVPN app on your computer.

To log into your account, click on the "Download Apps" button on the right side of the dashboard. Then, enter your username and password in the app. If you've forgotten your password, click the password reset link at the bottom of the login page. Select a new password and confirm it.

Once logged in, you can access the PureVPN dashboard from within the app. The dashboard provides a user-friendly interface for managing your VPN connection, including options for streaming, internet freedom, security and privacy, and more.

From the dashboard, you can also access the global map, which displays all available servers and their respective server counts. You can select a server by clicking on it and clicking the "Connect" button. This will establish a new VPN connection to the selected server.

Additionally, you can access advanced features in the settings menu of the app. These features include the ability to activate an internet kill switch, sync favorites across devices, and more. For most users, these advanced features will not be necessary, as the app offers a simple and streamlined interface for managing your VPN connection.

Mobile Setup

To set up PureVPN on your mobile device, download the PureVPN app from the App Store or Google Play Store.

Once installed, log in to your PureVPN account using your username and password. If you've forgotten your password, click the password reset link at the bottom of the login page. Select a new password and confirm it.

After logging in, you'll be presented with a quick tour of the app's features. Click on the bottom right button to access the list of available servers. Select a server in the United States and click "Install" to download the VPN configuration. Enter your phone passcode and click "Install" again.

Once installed, you can connect to PureVPN using the app. You can also connect from the Settings app on your mobile device. Click on the "Connect" button to establish a new VPN connection.

In the PureVPN app, you can access settings, including options for connecting to PPTP or L2TP, syncing favorites, and more. For most users, these settings will not be necessary, as the app offers a simple and streamlined interface for managing your VPN connection.

Conclusion

This tutorial has covered the basic setup and usage of PureVPN on both your computer and mobile device. We've discussed the installation process, logging in, and basic features of the app.

Remember, PureVPN is a popular VPN service that offers a wide range of features, including streaming, internet freedom, security and privacy, and more. With this tutorial, you should be able to get started with using PureVPN on your devices and exploring its features in more detail.
