AI Highlights This Week

Welcome to this week's AI highlights, where we'll cover the latest advancements in artificial intelligence.

Microsoft's Trellis 3D Model Generator

Trellis is a free and open-source 3D model generator that can create detailed models from simple text prompts. It can also upload images and generate 3D models based on the image.

  • Enter a prompt and Trellis will generate a 3D model
  • Upload an image and Trellis will generate a 3D model based on the image

Motion Prompting by Google DeepMind

Motion Prompting is a tool that allows you to animate images by dragging them on a screen. It can also understand the physics of real-world objects.

  • Drag an image to animate it
  • Understand the physics of real-world objects

Google's Gemini 2.0

Gemini 2.0 is a multimodal AI model that can understand text, images, audio, and video. It can also generate text, images, audio, or video.

  • Understand text, images, audio, and video
  • Generate text, images, audio, or video

Th0i AI Comic Generator

Th0i AI is a comic generator that can create comics from text descriptions. It can also customize characters and generate multiple pages.

  • Create comics from text descriptions
  • Customize characters
  • Generate multiple pages
  • Generate high-quality audio that syncs to any video
  • Add text prompts to guide the audio generation

Swift Edit is a free and open-source image editor that can edit images just by prompting it. It can also add additional prompts to the image.

  • Edit images just by prompting it
  • Add additional prompts to the image

Sora is a video generator that can create high-quality videos from text prompts. It can also do video-to-video generation.

  • Create high-quality videos from text prompts
  • Do video-to-video generation
