Google's Gemini Advanced 1.5 Pro Deep Research Tool

Google's Gemini Advanced 1.5 Pro Deep Research tool is a game-changer for anyone using the internet on a day-to-day basis. It's like Perplexity but on steroids, providing a vast amount of summarized information in a single document.

Activating the Tool

To activate the tool, navigate to the top left-hand side of the screen, select the drop-down menu, and ensure you are on 1.5 Pro with Deep Research. This will activate the tool, allowing you to access its advanced features.

Using the Tool

First, you'll need to determine what you want to research. Use an example document, such as looking up "autonomous vehicle sensor trends" for a robotics essay. Google will build a step-by-step plan to gather data around the web, providing you with six complete steps. Double-check the plan to ensure it meets your research goals.

Customizing the Plan

Once the plan is in place, you can edit it by clicking "Edit plan" and making adjustments as needed. This allows you to change the scope of your research, adding or removing steps to suit your needs.

Resulting Report

After activating the report, the tool will gather information from the web, creating a comprehensive report on the topic. This report includes a table and citations, allowing you to verify the sources and gain a deeper understanding of the topic.

Real-World Use Cases

The tool can be applied in various scenarios, such as researching stocks for investment purposes. A custom prompt can be created to retrieve specific data, such as top stocks showing unusual positive mentions, institutional buying patterns, and correlations between social media sentiment and price movement.

Conclusion

Google's Gemini Advanced 1.5 Pro Deep Research tool is an incredible resource, providing unparalleled access to research insights and saving a tremendous amount of time. By using the tool effectively, you can stay ahead in your research and gain a competitive edge in your chosen field.
