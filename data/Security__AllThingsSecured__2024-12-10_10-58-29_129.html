Create Better and More Secure Passwords

We all know that we need better passwords, but it feels like too much trouble. Thankfully, it doesn't have to be. In this guide, we'll walk you through a three-step process to create better and more secure passwords.

The 3-Step Process

  • Step 1: Select a Process to Create New Passwords

    Determine whether you'll use a manual method or a password manager to generate and store your passwords.

  • Step 2: Create a Way to Store Your Secure Passwords

    Choose a method to securely store your passwords, such as a password manager or a unique password for each account.

  • Step 3: Systematically Change Your Passwords

    Change your passwords for sensitive accounts every 6 months or less, and update your passwords for less sensitive accounts as needed.

Recommended Methods

Some recommended methods for creating secure passwords include:

  • Using a poem or song lyrics to generate a password
  • Creating a unique password for each account using a password manager
  • Using a password generator to create random and secure passwords

Using a Password Manager

Password managers like Dashlane can help you generate and store secure passwords, making it easier to protect your online identity.

Conclusion

Creating better and more secure passwords is a crucial step in protecting your online identity. By following the 3-step process outlined in this guide, you can create strong passwords and reduce the risk of identity theft.

Remember to regularly change your passwords for sensitive accounts and consider using a password manager to make the process easier and more secure.
