Locking Down Accounts with YubiKey

As a YubiKey owner, you may wonder what else you can do with it beyond securing your email account. In this article, we'll explore how to use your YubiKey to lock down four common apps and services.

Vanguard Account

To secure your Vanguard account, follow these steps:

  • Login to your Vanguard account and navigate to the profile and account settings page.
  • Scroll down and click on "Security" and then "Security Key".
  • Set up a security key, which is a second form of authentication, and name it.
  • Add a second security key, which will serve as your backup.
  • Once set up, you'll need to enter a temporary code sent to your phone to verify the key.
  • After verification, you'll be able to log in to your account using your username, password, and security key.

Apple iCloud Account

To secure your Apple iCloud account, follow these steps:

  • Open your settings app and navigate to your account settings page.
  • Click on "Two-Factor Authentication" and then "Security Keys".
  • Click on "Set Up" and enter your password to proceed.
  • Add your primary security key and name it.
  • Add your second security key and name it.
  • Review your active devices and add or remove as needed.
  • Exit two-factor authentication and make sure everything else in your iCloud account is locked down.

Facebook Account

To secure your Facebook account, follow these steps:

  • Open your Facebook account and navigate to the settings and privacy page.
  • Click on "Password and Security" and then select "Security Keys".
  • Choose your Facebook account and click on "Security Keys".
  • Register a new security key and plug it in to register your account.
  • Add a second security key if desired.
  • Review additional methods and set up recovery codes or other authentication options.
  • Lock down your Facebook account.

1Password Account

To secure your 1Password account, follow these steps:

  • Login to the 1Password web app and navigate to your profile page.
  • Click on "More Actions" and then "Manage 2FA".
  • Enter your password to confirm and then set up a new 2FA security key.
  • Add additional keys as needed.
  • Log in to your 1Password account and tap the security key to authenticate.
  • Repeat the process for your mobile device.

By following these steps, you can lock down your Vanguard, Apple iCloud, Facebook, and 1Password accounts using your YubiKey security key.
