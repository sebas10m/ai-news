A List of Useful AI Tools for Content Creation and Productivity

Napkin AI is a free tool for creating visuals for video content, newsletters, blogs, presentations, and social media posts. It has a user-friendly interface and a wide range of styles to choose from. Google's Notebook LM is another free AI tool that can generate summaries, podcasts, and study guides based on text.

Pinocchio is a platform for downloading open-source AI tools that simplifies the process of setting up and using these models. Replicate is a cloud-based solution for testing and using AI models that offers a pay-by-usage model. Spotter Studio is a tool for brainstorming video ideas and titles, as well as finding the best-performing videos on YouTube.

Guide is a tool for creating step-by-step guides and tutorials that can be shared with others. Asuno is a music generation tool that offers a feature for uploading audio and extending it. These tools have been extremely useful for me and have saved me a ton of time.
