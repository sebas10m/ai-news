What to Expect from OpenAI in 2025

In 2025, OpenAI is expected to make significant advancements in AI technology, including voice mode changes, memory enhancements, and a potential hardware play. Here are some of the key things we can expect from OpenAI in the coming year.

Voice Mode Changes and Memory Enhancements

Sam Altman, the head of OpenAI, has discussed the need for better voice mode changes and memory enhancements in AI technology. These advancements will allow for more accurate and seamless conversations with AI models, as well as improved retention of previous conversations.

Hardware Play

OpenAI is also working on a hardware play, which will allow for more advanced and efficient AI processing. This will enable a wide range of new applications and use cases for AI technology.

Proper Agent

OpenAI is also working on a proper agent, which will be a research preview released in January. This agent will be able to take actions on a person's behalf, such as writing code or booking travel.

Model Improvements

OpenAI's Jason Wei has stated that progress in AI model improvements will be much faster in the coming year, with potentially four iteration cycles in terms of model improvements. This means that by the end of 2025, we can expect to see significant advancements in AI technology, including models that are much more intelligent and capable than current ones.
