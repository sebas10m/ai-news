Dashlane Review

Dashlane is a time-tested password manager app that offers a wide range of features to help users create and store better passwords.

Features

  • Securely syncs all passwords, IDs, credit card information, and notes across multiple devices
  • Offers excellent browser extensions
  • Allows secure sharing of passwords
  • Provides an emergency contact feature
  • Offers a free version with limited features
  • Includes an auto login feature
  • Provides password health scoring and recommendations
  • Offers a virtual private network (VPN) feature
  • Supports two-factor authentication

Business Features

Dashlane is well-suited for business users due to its ease of use, high security, and ability to control and monitor employee passwords.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Dashlane is a well-designed password manager app that offers a wide range of features to help users create and store better passwords. While it may not be the cheapest option, its unique features and high security make it a great choice for individual and corporate users.
