NVIDIA's Game-Changing Supercomputer: A Tiny, Affordable, and Powerful Device

NVIDIA has just released a game-changing supercomputer that is tiny, affordable, and incredibly powerful.

This supercomputer can deliver large language models to the edge more than any other device, making it possible for open-source models to run independently anywhere.

NVIDIA CEO on the Jetson Orin Supercomputer

NVIDIA's CEO explains that the Jetson Orin is a micro supercomputer that is blazingly fast at running inference on the edge and can run large language models, essential for robots, Internet of Things devices, and cars.

The Jetson Orin can run 275 trillion operations per second, making it an incredible computer that can essentially run anything, from robots to Internet of Things devices.

Emergence AI Partnership

NVIDIA partnered with Emergence AI to demonstrate the capabilities of their multi-agent orchestrator, which can browse the web on behalf of users, making it easier to use and more efficient.

Emergence AI's orchestrator offers a combination of design time flexibility and runtime determinism, allowing agents to heal themselves and run complex tasks without human intervention.

Jetson Orin Performance and Features

The Jetson Orin has a 6-core ARM Cortex CPU, 1,024 CUDA cores, 32 Tensor cores, and 8 GB of fast memory, making it a powerful device that can run large language models and vision models.

The Orin's performance is impressive, with large language models running extremely well, and it can power vision models and vision transformers, essential for autonomous driving and other applications.
