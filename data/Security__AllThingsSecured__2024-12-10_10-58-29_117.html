Setting Up Google Titan Security Key

In this video, we will walk through the process of setting up the Google Titan Security Key to secure your online accounts.

Step 1: Unboxing and Preparing the Security Key

First, we will open the box and take out the key, USB cable, and other accessories provided with the Google Titan Security Key.

Step 2: Setting Up Two-Step Verification on Google

Log in to your Google account, go to your Google account settings, and click on '2-Step Verification'. Scroll down and click on 'Add security key'.

Step 3: Registering the Security Key

Register the security key by plugging it in and tapping the button to allow. Name the key, for example, 'Josh's wireless key'.

Step 4: Setting Up the Security Key on Your Phone

Download the Google Smart Locker app and use it to sign in to your account. Press the button on the key fob until the blue light starts blinking, then pair the key with your phone.

Enter the digit on the back of the device on your phone, and you will be secure to sign in. You can now log in to your online accounts, such as Gmail or Facebook, using the security key.

Congratulations, you have set up your Google Titan Security Key!

This process of setting up a security key can be applied to various online applications, not just Gmail or Facebook.
