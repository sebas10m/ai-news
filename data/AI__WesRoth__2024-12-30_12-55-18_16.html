Demis Hassabis on the Future of AI in Biology and beyond

Recently, Demis Hassabis, the co-founder of DeepMind, spoke about the potential of AI in biology and beyond. In his speech, he discussed how AI can help us uncover the secrets of biology and even allow us to engineer life.

In his speech, Hassabis highlighted the significance of AlphaFold, a machine learning system that can predict the 3D structure of proteins with high accuracy. He explained that this achievement has the potential to accelerate the discovery of new medicines, treatments, and even new biological systems.

Hassabis also emphasized the importance of understanding the limits of classical learning algorithms, which are a type of AI system that can learn from data without requiring quantum computation.
