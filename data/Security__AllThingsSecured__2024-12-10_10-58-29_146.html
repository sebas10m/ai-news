Progress Over Perfection: A Mindset Shift for Private Cybersecurity

As we strive for better privacy online, it's essential to shift our mindset from seeking perfection to making progress. Instead of focusing on complete invisibility and untraceability, let's prioritize being intentional about who controls our online lives.

Small Priorities, Simple Habits, and Helpful Tools

  • Using better tools, such as password managers, encrypted email, email aliases, and physical security keys like YubiKeys
  • Building habits like reconciling bank accounts and reviewing statements regularly
  • Communicating intentionally, sharing personal information thoughtfully, and browsing the internet responsibly

Your Personal Privacy Priorities

Before taking drastic measures, ask yourself: does this align with my personal privacy priorities? What exactly am I trying to protect and from whom?

The Value of Small Wins

Taking small steps toward better habits, tools, and mindset will help you avoid stressing about privacy and security. As Ecclesiastes says, 'sow your seed in the morning and at evening let your hands not be idle for you do not know which will succeed.'
