IPVanish Review

IPVanish is a virtual private network that has been around since 2012. In this comprehensive review, we'll take a look at the desktop and mobile apps, features, and ownership model.

Desktop and Mobile Apps

The IPVanish desktop app has a simple layout with a connect button on the top right and a list of servers on the bottom right. The mobile app is similar, with a connect button on the bottom right and a list of servers on the upper left-hand corner.

Features

  • 256-bit encryption
  • 1400 servers spread across 50 countries
  • VPN kill switch
  • Zero log policy
  • Standalone apps for all major platforms
  • 10 simultaneous connections
  • 250 gigabytes of free cloud storage through SugarSync
  • Great customer support, including email, live chat, and phone support

Ownership Model

IPVanish is owned by NetProtect, which is a company that owns several other VPN properties. In 2019, NetProtect was acquired by J2 Global, a publicly traded technology company based in the US.

This ownership model can be seen as both good and bad. On the one hand, having a publicly traded company as the owner can provide additional transparency and accountability. On the other hand, some may be concerned about the potential for data sharing and security risks.

Conclusion

IPVanish has all the standard features expected from a VPN, plus some unique features like 10 simultaneous connections and 250 gigabytes of free cloud storage. While the software itself could use a refresh, the company's customer support and ownership model are major positives.
