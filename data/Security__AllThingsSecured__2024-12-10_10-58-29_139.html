Streaming on Apple TV with SmartDNS

Apple does not allow for VPN configurations on an Apple TV. However, you can stream services like Netflix and Hulu using SmartDNS.

Setting up ExpressVPN

Sign in to your ExpressVPN account and go to the settings section. Select "Apple TV" and note down the DNS server IP address.

Configuring Apple TV

Go to the home screen of your Apple TV and navigate to the "Settings" app. Select "General" and then your wireless network.

  • Select "Manual DNS" and input the DNS server IP address from ExpressVPN.
  • Confirm the settings and wait for the Apple TV to apply the changes.
  • Restart your Apple TV to ensure the settings take effect.

Once the Apple TV has restarted, you should be able to stream services like Netflix and Hulu without any issues.

This process has worked well for me using ExpressVPN and their SmartDNS settings.
