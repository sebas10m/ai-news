Hackathon Registration and Rules

Competition Overview

The Automator hackathon is a competition where you can showcase your skills in building an AI agent for the Automator Live Agent Studio. The competition will run from January 1 to January 22, with registration open from now until January 8.

Prizes

  • The best AI agent overall will win $1,500 and a special badge on the live agent studio.
  • The second place winner will win $1,000 and a special badge on the studio.
  • The third place winner will win a special badge and $500.
  • Runner-ups will each win $150.
  • The best and second-best Voiceflow agents will win $700 and $300, respectively.

Timeline

Registration opens now and will run until January 8.

  • The competition will officially start on January 8 and will run until January 22.
  • Submissions will close on January 22.
  • The community will vote on the top agents from January 26 to February 1.
  • The winners will be announced on February 1 at 9 o'clock Central Time.

Judging Criteria

The initial round of judging will evaluate agents based on the following criteria:

  • Innovation: How novel is the idea?
  • Functionality: How well does the agent actually work?
  • Usability: How easy is it to use and implement?
  • Presentation: How well is the agent presented through documentation and video?
