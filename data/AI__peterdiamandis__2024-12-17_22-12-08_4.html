Workplace Evolution: A New Way Beyond Work from Home or Return to Office

Companies are exploring a third way of working beyond the traditional work from home or return to office models.

Technology Enables Flexibility

Advances in technology have made it possible to work remotely, but it's not just about technology. Companies need to consider the needs and preferences of employees.

The Three C's: Collaboration, Customer, and Community

  • Collaboration brings together different disciplines, allowing teams to work together seamlessly.
  • Embedding employees with customers enables a deeper understanding of their needs and allows for more effective teamwork.
  • Community is essential, allowing employees to work in a location that suits them best and enable them to thrive.

Amazon's Return to Office Policy

Amazon's return to office policy is forcing people to return, and other companies are seeing the benefits of this. Employees who don't want to return to the office are being lured away by companies that offer flexible working arrangements.

Traditional Thinking and Sunk Costs

Many large companies are hesitant to adopt remote work arrangements due to traditional thinking and sunk costs in infrastructure such as office buildings.

A New Approach to Work

A key aspect of a successful remote work arrangement is trust. Employees need to be trusted to manage themselves and work towards key objectives.
