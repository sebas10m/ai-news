AI News Update

Elon Musk's prediction for AGI is estimated to be 2026 at the latest. Several companies have released new tools and updates for AI development. Black Forest Lab has released a suite of models, Flux.1 Tools, designed to add control and steerability to their base text-to-image model Flux1. Figure Robotics has released a new video update showcasing their humanoid robots working in BMW factories, boasting improved speed and accuracy.

Text-to-Image Models

  • Black Forest Lab's Flux.1 Tools enables modification and recreation of real and generated images.
  • The first tool, Flux.1 Fill, is a state-of-the-art in-painting and out-painting model.
  • Other tools include Flux.1 Depth, Flux.1 Canny, and Flux.1 Redux.

Large Language Models

  • Quen has extended its context window to a million tokens and increased inference speed.
  • Quen 2.5 Turbo achieves a 100% accuracy on finding information from million tokens.
  • 11 Labs now allows users to create conversational agents with customizable variables.

Geospatial Models

  • Niantic has published a geospatial AI model using scans of the world from Pokemon Go and Ingress.
  • The model can navigate the world like ChatGPT spits out text.

Music and Audio

  • Suno V4 enables users to make any song they can imagine with better audio, sharper lyrics, and more dynamic song structures.
  • Suno V4 includes features such as remastering, creating lyrics, and making custom cover art.

ChatGPT and Gemini

  • ChatGPT now has advanced voice mode for desktop users.
  • Gemini's Advanced API can remember users' interests and preferences for more helpful, relevant responses.

Open Source Thinking Model

Deep Seek has released an open-source thinking model that can take its time to think and provide the best answer possible.

Quantum Computing

  • Google DeepMind has released AlphaCubit, a model that predicts errors in quantum computing.
  • This could be the last unlock necessary to see quantum computers in everyday life.

GPT-4 Update

  • GPT-4's creative writing ability has leveled up, allowing for more natural, engaging, and tailored writing.
  • The model is also better at working with uploaded files and providing deeper insights and more thorough responses.
