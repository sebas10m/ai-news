Amazon Nova: State-of-the-Art Foundation Models

Amazon Nova is a set of state-of-the-art foundation models that deliver frontier intelligence and industry-leading price performance.

Four Flavors of Models

  • Micro: A text-only model that inputs text and outputs text, with a focus on simplicity and cost-effectiveness.
  • Light: A multimodal model that inputs text, image, or video and outputs text, with a balance of size and intelligence.
  • Pro: A multimodal model that inputs text, image, or video and outputs text, with a focus on higher intelligence and fine-tuning capabilities.
  • Premier: A large multimodal model that inputs text, image, or video and outputs text, speech, images, or video, available in Q1 timeframe.

Benchmarks and Comparison

The Nova models have been benchmarked against leading models, with competitive results on various metrics.

  • Micro model compares favorably to LAMA and Google's Gemini on raw numbers and statistical significance testing.
  • Light model compared to OpenAI's GPT-40 Mini shows similar performance.
  • Pro model compares favorably to GPT-40 and Haiku on various benchmarks.
  • Premier model coming in Q1 timeframe will be the largest multimodal model.

Cost-Effective and Fast

The Nova models are designed to be highly cost-effective and fast, with a latency-optimized inference SKU available.

Integrated with Bedrock

The Nova models are deeply integrated with Bedrock, enabling fine-tuning, distillation, and other advanced features.

Announcing Nova Canvas and Reel

Amazon is launching two new models: Nova Canvas for image generation and Nova Reel for video generation.

  • Crossley outperforms DALI 3 and Stable Diffusion 3.5 on image quality and instruction following.
  • Reel generates stunning videos with control over the camera, motion, and AI controls for safe AI.

Future Plans

Amazon plans to develop the next generation of Nova models and introduce a speech-to-speech model and an any-to-any model in the future.

AWS's Model Strategy

AWS will provide customers with the broadest and best functionality in the market, with the ability to use various models for different needs and purposes.

Conclusion

Amazon Nova has made a significant stride in the AI market by introducing its own frontier models that compare favorably to leading AI companies.

With its state-of-the-art foundation models and integrated features, Amazon Nova is poised to become a key player in the AI ecosystem.
