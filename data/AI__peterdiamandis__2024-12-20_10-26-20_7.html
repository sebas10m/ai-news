2025 Predictions: AI, Bitcoin, Space, and More

Join Peter Diamandis and Salim Ismail, CEO of OpenEXO, as they discuss their predictions for 2025 in various tech sectors.

Bitcoin

Peter predicts Bitcoin will reach $300,000 by the end of 2025, citing institution buy-ins and strategic reserves.

Salim notes the deflation of the dollar and the potential volatility of Bitcoin, but emphasizes its ability to recover from shocks.

AI and AGI

Peter predicts AI will reach IQs of 140 in 2025, with Grok 3, GPT-5, and Anthropix Clawed 3.5 making significant progress.

Salim believes AI will continue to improve, potentially leading to AGI, but notes the need for a clear definition and test for consciousness.

Humanoid Robots

Peter predicts humanoid robots will become increasingly popular, with companies like MetaTrend and HeyGen making significant advancements.

Salim notes the importance of considering the social and emotional implications of humanoid robots, citing the need for a massive new area of study.

EVs and Autonomous Cars

Peter discusses the rapid progress in EVs and autonomous cars, citing companies like Waymo and Tesla.

Salim predicts a tipping point has been reached, and the cost of driving should drop significantly in the coming years.

BCI

Peter discusses the latest advancements in brain-computer interfaces (BCI), including the mapping of a fruit fly's connectome.

Salim notes the potential for BCI to revolutionize human cognition, allowing for the offloading of processing and freeing up neurons for other tasks.
