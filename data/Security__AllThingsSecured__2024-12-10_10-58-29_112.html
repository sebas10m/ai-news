ExpressVPN Review: Is it Trustworthy and Secure?

Welcome to All Things Secured. My name's Josh, and as a longtime user of ExpressVPN, I'm here to give you my two cents on the service.

Ease of Use

ExpressVPN has made significant strides in making their software accessible to users of all technical backgrounds. The power button, a feature they pioneered, makes connecting to the VPN a simple process.

Security and Trust

ExpressVPN's claim of not logging user data is supported by their track record, which includes withstanding a server seizure during the investigation of the Russian ambassador to Turkey's assassination.

Owners and Operation

ExpressVPN co-founder Peter Burkhart is a German-American entrepreneur who has worked for Microsoft in China and has a background in business. However, little information is publicly available about the company's ownership and operations.

Conclusion

While ExpressVPN has made significant strides in making their software accessible and secure, the lack of transparency about their ownership and operations makes it difficult to fully trust the service.
