Flux Tools Depth and Canny Workflow

Control nets have been available for quite some time, but there are some new players on the market. This workflow uses the official Black Forest Labs Depth and Canny models for Flux.

The Workflow

The workflow involves loading two diffusion models, one for depth and one for canny. The image is then processed in two passes, first with the depth model and then with the canny model. The control net strength can be adjusted to fine-tune the output.

  1. Loading the models: FB18, Canny, and Depth
  2. Adjusting the control net strength: 20 steps
  3. Swapping between canny and depth: switch case
