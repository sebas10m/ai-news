Introducing HyperWrite: The AI Assistant for Personal Tasks

HyperWrite is a Chrome plugin that offers an AI writing companion like Jasper, but with a new feature called AI assistant. This AI assistant has access to your entire browser, allowing you to perform tasks such as booking flights, sending emails, and even generating leads on LinkedIn.

Use Cases

Email Management

HyperWrite can read and respond to unread emails on Gmail, making it easier to manage your inbox. You can ask it to respond to all unread emails, and it will send out responses on your behalf.

Lead Generation on LinkedIn

The AI assistant can find posts about generative AI on LinkedIn and leave comments on each post, warming up the connection before reaching out to potential customers.

Reviewing Pull Requests on GitHub

HyperWrite can review pull requests for you, leaving comments and notifications, making it easier to manage code changes.

Writing Blog Posts and Tweets

The AI assistant can write and publish blog posts, including tweets, on your behalf, making it easier to maintain your online presence.

Tech Details

HyperWrite is currently in alpha 0.01, but it has already impressive results. The AI assistant has access to Google Chrome plugins, allowing it to perform tasks such as executing browser actions, sending emails, and generating leads on LinkedIn.

Conclusion

HyperWrite is a powerful AI assistant that can perform a wide range of tasks, from email management to lead generation on LinkedIn, reviewing pull requests on GitHub, and even writing blog posts and tweets. While it still has some limitations, it is an exciting development in the field of AI and personal assistants.
