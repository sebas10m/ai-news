OpenAI Announces O3 Model

OpenAI has announced their latest model called O3, which showed impressive results in various benchmarks.

In software engineering, O3 was 71.7% accurate compared to O1, while in competition code, it outscored O1 Preview and O1.

O3 also performed well in competition math, research math, and PhD-level science, with accuracy rates of 96.7%, 25.2%, and 87.7%, respectively.

AGI Definition by Microsoft and OpenAI

Microsoft and OpenAI have their own internal definition of AGI, which is tied to the company's financial performance.

According to the agreement, AGI will be achieved when OpenAI develops systems that can generate $100 billion in profits for its earliest investors, including Microsoft.

Sam Altman's Feedback on ChatGPT

Sam Altman, the CEO of OpenAI, has been gathering feedback on ChatGPT from the public on X.

Some of the suggestions include a vector store for the API, a video input modality, a hardware play, and a drag-and-drop UI for multiple chat models.
