Infinite Email Addresses: A Game-Changer for Gmail Control

You can modify your Gmail address to create unique email addresses without creating multiple accounts. By adding a period or plus sign before the @ symbol, you can create a new email address that still gets delivered to your inbox.

  • Adding a period before the @ symbol, e.g., j.ondo@}
  • Using the plus sign before the @ symbol with a word, e.g., johndo+werk@}

These modified email addresses can be used to create filters within Gmail, which can sort and direct emails to specific folders or labels.

  • Organize newsletters and create filters to skip the inbox
  • Use a specific email address to create a to-do list and reminders
  • Set up automated tasks and notifications using if-then logic
  • Designate VIP email addresses to receive notifications and direct messages

By leveraging infinite email addresses, you can take control of your Gmail inbox and customize your email experience.
