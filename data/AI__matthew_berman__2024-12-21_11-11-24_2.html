New Territory in AGI

OpenAI has released O3, a new frontier model that surpasses all previous models in performance and capabilities.

AGI Achieved?

AMAZING Results

O3 has achieved a flawless score of 96.7% on the competition math benchmark and 87.7% on the PhD-level science questions benchmark (GPQA diamond).
