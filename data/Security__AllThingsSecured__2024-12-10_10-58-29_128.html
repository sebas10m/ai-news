Setting Up a Passkey on Amazon

To set up a passkey on Amazon, follow these steps:

Using 1Password or a Password Manager

1. Go to your Amazon account settings and click on "Login & Security."

2. Click on "Set up" next to "Passkey."

3. Choose your password manager (e.g., 1Password) and select "New item."

4. Save the passkey to your account.

Using iCloud Keychain

1. Go to your Amazon account settings and click on "Login & Security."

2. Click on "Set up" next to "Passkey."

3. Choose "iCloud Keychain" and select "New password."

4. Save the passkey to your iCloud keychain.

Using a YubiKey or Physical Key

1. Create a pin code for your YubiKey or physical key.

2. Go to your Amazon account settings and click on "Login & Security."

3. Click on "Set up" next to "Passkey."

4. Choose "Security key" and select "Add." Enter your pin code and allow the security key to use a passkey.

Logging In with a Passkey

When logging in to Amazon, you will be prompted to enter your passkey. This can be done using the 1Password extension, iCloud keychain, or YubiKey/physical key.

Best Practices

* Always use a unique pin code for your YubiKey or physical key.

* Consider creating multiple keys for backup purposes.

* Use a password manager to securely store your passkeys.
