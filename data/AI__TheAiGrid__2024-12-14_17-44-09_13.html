Creating Scary Videos with AI Agents

The article discusses the process of creating scary videos using a team of AI agents. The author uses a combination of tools, including Make.com, GPT-4, and 11 Labs, to generate a script, images, and a voiceover for a scary story.

Using AI Agents to Create Videos

The author explains how they use a team of AI agents to create videos, including a script generator, image generator, and voiceover generator. They use Make.com to create an automation workflow that connects these agents and generates a final video product.

Generating a Script and Images

The author shares a prompt they use to generate a script for a scary story, which includes details about the setting, characters, and plot. They then use GPT-4 to generate images for each section of the story.

Creating a Voiceover

The author uses 11 Labs to generate a voiceover for the story, which they then store in a file. They explain how they choose a voice and configure the settings to ensure the voiceover sounds good.

Assembling the Final Video

The author shares their workflow for assembling the final video, which involves importing the generated script, images, and voiceover into video editing software and adding polishing touches, such as film grains and slow zoom effects.

Conclusion

The author concludes by explaining that creating scary videos with AI agents is a relatively simple process that can be done with minimal technical expertise. They invite viewers to leave comments with specific automation requests and to check out their community for more resources.
