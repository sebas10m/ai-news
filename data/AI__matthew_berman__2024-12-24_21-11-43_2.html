Reactions to O3

O3 was announced a few days ago, and the industry's reaction has been incredible.

Frontier Math Benchmark

O3 achieved an impressive score of 25% on the frontier math benchmark, shattering the previous record.

  • Terence Tao, a Fields medalist, said the problems are "extremely challenging" and require a combination of human expertise and AI analysis.
  • Gowers, another Fields medalist, described the problems as "at a different level of difficulty" and "hard to solve."

Ethan Malek's Reaction

Ethan Malek, a professor at Wharton, compared O3's achievement to Douglas Adams' prediction of a supercomputer taking 7.5 million years to calculate the answer to the ultimate question of life.

ARC Benchmarks Founder Reaction

Francois Chalet, the creator of the ARK benchmark, said O3's achievement represents a significant breakthrough in getting AI to adapt to novel tasks.

However, he also pointed out that O3 still fails on simple logic and reasoning problems and that it's not sustainable in its current form.

Emad's Reaction

Emad, the former founder and CEO of Stability AI, said "the global economy is cooked" and that a new economic and societal framework is needed.

Scientific Community Reaction

Daria Unutmez, a professor and biomedical scientist, was impressed by O1 Pro's ability to critically evaluate a review on immune cells and their role in cancer.

He said the industry is shocked by O1 Pro's insights and that it's a topic where he's one of the few top experts in the world.

Other Reactions

  • Noam Brown, researching multi-agent reasoning at OpenAI, said the compressed timeline of major step function advances in AI is remarkable.
  • David Dohan from OpenAI shared that O3 thought for 16 hours to get a 3.5% increase in score per hour, which is an insane amount of time and tokens used.
  • Yann LeCun predicted that using ARK and Chain of Thought would lead to worse results, but it seems he was wrong.
  • Gary Marcus said O3 will be a hype phenomenon, but reliable in closed domains like math problems, not in open-ended domains like everyday reasoning.

Industry Reactions

  • Ben Thompson, founder of Stratechery, said O3 is a big deal, but it's downstream of the O1 revelation that inference time scaling matters.
  • Tammei, at Epoch AI Research, was genuinely impressed by OpenAI's 25.2% pass at Frontier Math.
  • Sam Altman said O3 mini will outperform O1 at a massive cost reduction.
  • Dr. Jim Phan from NVIDIA said O3 is a super intelligence in certain domains, but still fails to solve simple puzzles like the ones O3 failed on.
