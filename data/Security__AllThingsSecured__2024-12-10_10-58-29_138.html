Putting a VPN on an Amazon Fire TV Stick

Having a VPN on your Amazon Fire TV stick allows you to access geo-restricted content while traveling.

To do this, you'll need to download and install a virtual private network (VPN) app, such as ExpressVPN, from Amazon Fire TV.com.

Once installed, you'll need to insert your username and password to access the app's servers.

With ExpressVPN, you'll have access to hundreds of servers worldwide, allowing you to watch content from different platforms, including ABC, ESPN, HBO, and Netflix.
